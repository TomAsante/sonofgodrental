Who we are and what we do


Son Of God is specialised in hire equipment for events, we offer a wide range of high-quality event equipment available for hire.
Our equipment is suitable for all types of celebrations such as weddings, religious, corporate events and parties.
Our extensive equipment includes sound equipment, lighting equipment and audiovisual equipment as well as power generators folding chairs and event marquees which we can tailor to meet your specific event needs.
Whether your event is large or small we provide a professional service at affordable prices.
If you donít find your requirements in any of the above events, donít worry. Get in touch with us and we will help find a solution tailored to your needs and specification.
